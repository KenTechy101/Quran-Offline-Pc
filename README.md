## 📚 About Quran-Offline-Pc

Quran-Offline-Pc is an open-source program for reading, listening, and understanding the Quran on your Windows computer. It works without an internet connection. The app combines audio recitations, clear text, and simple explanations to make the Quran accessible for everyone.

You do not need any special skills or tools. Just download it and start using it right away. The app stores all data locally on your PC, so you can study and search the Quran anytime.

Topics included in the app cover various areas like Quran audio, Quran text display, offline data storage, and simple search features. It offers a clean way to interact with the Quran without distractions or the need for a web connection.

## 🎧 Using Quran-Offline-Pc

When you open the app, you will see a simple interface with three main sections:

- **Text Viewer:** Displays the Quran in clear Arabic script along with simple translations and explanations.  
- **Audio Player:** Plays Quran recitations in high quality. You can pause, rewind, or skip to specific chapters.  
- **Search Panel:** Type any word or phrase to find its location in the Quran text or explanations.

You can adjust font size and the audio volume to your preference. The app also allows bookmarking verses for easy return later.

## 🔍 Searching and Exploring the Quran

The app lets you search for Quranic text or words anywhere in the collection. This feature works offline and is fast.

Steps to search:

1. Open the search box from the main screen.  
2. Type the word or phrase you want to find.  
3. Press "Enter" or click the search button.  
4. View the results showing verse numbers and relevant explanations.  
5. Click on any result to jump directly to that part of the Quran.

This tool helps you study or revisit topics quickly without scrolling through the entire text.
